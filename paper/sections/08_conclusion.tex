We demonstrate statistically significant ($p<0.001$) architectural advantages that favor generic Lattice systems over recursive hash trees across almost all constrained configurations.

We established empirically that Class 0 endpoints absolutely demand distinct deployment interventions bypassing native PQC payloads entirely directly corroborating physical measurements recorded globally.
